Welcome to Adopt-A-Pet! This website will allow you to adopt a cat or a dog of your choice! You can either look at all the pets that are available for adoption in this center in "Browse Available Pets", fill out a forms on your desired pet in "Find A Dog/Cat", look at instructions on how to take care of your pet in either "Dog Care" or "Cat Care", fill out a form to give up a pet for adoption in "Have a Pet Give Away", or, if you have any questions, you can also send us a message in "Contact Us".
